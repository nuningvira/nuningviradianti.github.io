History of STBA Pontianak

by Nuning Suardianti Alfira, November 1, 2021

STBA Pontianak Indonesia is the first campus with a major in English Literature in West Kalimantan.

In 1994, the establishment of the Pontianak Foreign Language Academy / ABA held the English Three Diploma Program. In 2006, ABA Pontianak obtained an accreditation score of "B (Good)" BAN-PT, then ABA Pontianak again obtained an accreditation score of "B (Good)" BAN-PT in 2014. Based on the Decree of the Minister of Education and Culture of the Republic of Indonesia No. 590/E/0/2014 dated October 17, 2014, ABA Pontianak was entrusted by the Director-General of Higher Education to organize the English Literature Study Program (Bachelor Program/S1) and subsequently changed its form to the College of Foreign Languages / STBA Pontianak.

Starting from 2015, STBA Pontianak officially organizes the English Literature Study Program (Bachelor Program / S1) and the English Language Study Program (Three Diploma Program / DIII)

All about STBA Pontianak

STBA Pontianak has 5 comfortable study rooms equipped with air conditioning (AC) in each room.

The main campus of STBA Pontianak is located on Jl. Gajahmada No.49.

Its strategic location in the middle of the city makes the STBA Pontianak campus easy to find.
